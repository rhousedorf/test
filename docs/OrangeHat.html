Orange Hat tomato

 

Extra-dwarf bush plants reach just 6-9 inches in height, depending on fertilization.

The plants are prolific, offering plenty of small tomatoes that burst with fruity, sweet flavor. 

PLEASE be sure to let them fully ripen!

Suggested as an indoor or patio tomato, comfortably fitting in a 4 to 6-inch pot.

Try these colorful and compact plants as an edible ornamental, mass planted in beds or for borders. 
BEWARE OF DEER!!  If you actually want to eat the fruit...

Perfect for sunny windows and with grow lights!

If kept in a suitable pot and taken care of, no freezing, proper watering anf fertilizing, ETC, ETC, this plant should produce for years.

This is my year growing, and selling, these, so we'll see just how disease resistant they are.

From the seed source:

  • Determinate
  • Full Sun
  • Sprouts in 7-14 Days
  • Ideal Temperature: 75-95 Degrees F
  • Plant Spacing: 8"
  • Frost Hardy: No
